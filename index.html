I am an Associate Professor at the University of Texas at Austin LBJ School of Public Affairs and Department of Educational Leadership and Policy, a NBER Research Associate, an IZA Research Fellow, and a CESifo Research Affiliate.

My research focuses on the economics of education — with particular emphasis on student loans, college completion, financial aid, and how institutions respond to policy changes.
